About Us

At OBS Cafe, we believe every great day starts with a great cup of coffee. What once was a butcher’s corner is now a rustic café where heritage and modern taste meet.

  • We grind fresh beans daily for a rich, Aromatic experience.
  • Our pastries are handmade with local ingredients.
  • Friendly baristas greet you like family — every time.
  • Free Wi-Fi and cozy corners for study, work, or reflection.
  • Seasonal specials inspired by local flavors.
  • We use biodegradable cups and eco packaging.
